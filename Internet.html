As a creative multimedia student, having a fast and reliable Internet service is important because it helps them to download or transfer big projects from and to the Internet. In this modern world, there are many local Internet service provider that can be found offering various Internet plans for the public to subscribe from them. As techonology advances from time to time, the pricing of Internet plans reduces.

When it comes in choosing the best Internet plan for students, there are a few things that must be taken into consideration. Things that should be taken into consideration:
-Internet Coverage: Does the my housing area supports the Internet speed?
-Pricing: Am I able to afford that Internet plan? Is it worth the asking price? Is there other cheaper plans?
-After sale service: Do the company entertain to customer's complain? How good is the company reputation?
-Internet Speed: Which Internet speed is the best for me? Is the more the better? Do I need unlimited Internet quota?

Using the tips above can help students to decide which Internet plan that suits them the best. But if students still can't decide, they can check my recommended Internet plan at the recommendation page.
