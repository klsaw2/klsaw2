Creative Multimedia Creation Program

Welcome to the Creative Multimedia Creation programme in Disted College! In this programme, you will develop the necessary skills, knowledge, and inovative thinking for creative and multimedia communication in art and design, marketing, entertainment, education and many many more. Besides that, you will also be equipped with a comprehensive education in the principles and practices of multimedia design, production, management and evaluation.

Duration: 2 years and 5 months( including sem breaks, exams and internship )

Price: RM23,000( Locals ) RM27,000( Overseas Students )
